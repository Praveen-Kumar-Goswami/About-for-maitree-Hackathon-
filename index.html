About Maitree

Maitree is a pioneering rural healthcare app designed to bridge the critical gap between patients and emergency services. As the first of its kind in this segment, Maitree redefines accessibility and response time in rural healthcare delivery.

With smart digital integration, Maitree minimizes delays in patient–ambulance coordination, ensuring that medical help reaches faster than ever before. Its dedicated emergency button for drivers enables immediate alerts and quick response during critical situations, making every second count.

By digitalizing key healthcare processes, Maitree streamlines communication, documentation, and response systems — effectively cutting down time, effort, and confusion during emergencies.

Maitree — your trusted companion for faster, smarter, and more connected rural healthcare.
