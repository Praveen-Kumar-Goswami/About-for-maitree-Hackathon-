How Maitree Helps

Designed specifically for rural communities with limited healthcare access

Fast Ambulance Service

Get immediate access to the nearest available ambulance, saving 20-40 minutes during emergencies. Direct connection from user to ambulance and then to the most appropriate hospital.

Medicine Reminders

Never miss a dose with smart medication reminders tailored to your treatment schedule. Set custom alerts for different medications.

Digital Health Records

Securely store and access your medical history, prescriptions, and test results anytime, anywhere. Share with healthcare providers when needed.
