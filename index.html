About Maitree

Maitree is a pioneering rural healthcare app designed to bridge the critical gap between patients and emergency services, especially in underserved and remote areas. As the first of its kind in this segment, Maitree redefines accessibility, improving response time, and ensuring healthcare services are closer to those who need them most.

In rural communities, delays in accessing timely healthcare can be life-threatening. Maitree addresses this issue by providing a real-time digital platform that connects patients directly with emergency services such as ambulances and healthcare professionals, minimizing the time it takes for help to arrive.

Key features include a user-friendly emergency button for drivers, which sends immediate alerts to the nearest healthcare facilities and ambulances. This critical functionality ensures faster response times during high-pressure situations, where every second counts. The app also enables real-time tracking of ambulance locations, giving families peace of mind and better situational awareness.

In addition, Maitree integrates essential healthcare processes like documentation and communication, allowing healthcare providers to receive critical patient information ahead of time. This integration helps reduce errors, confusion, and time delays, ensuring that the medical team is prepared as soon as they arrive on the scene.

Maitree’s mission is to bring smarter, more connected healthcare solutions to rural areas, empowering both patients and healthcare providers to respond faster and more effectively. Whether it’s a minor emergency or a life-threatening situation, Maitree is committed to making rural healthcare more accessible, reliable, and efficient.

Maitree — your trusted companion for faster, smarter, and more connected rural healthcare.
